============================================================
  HOW TO ADD YOUR 2 PHOTOS TO "THE PIC I LOVED MOSTLY"
============================================================

STEP 1 — Find this folder:
   Open File Explorer and go to:
   C:\Users\PURNA - REVANTH\OneDrive\Documents\Kumar\assets\

STEP 2 — Rename your first photo:
   Take your first favourite picture of her.
   Rename the image file to exactly:
       photo1.jpg
   (it can also be .png — if so, see Step 4)

STEP 3 — Rename your second photo:
   Take your second favourite picture of her.
   Rename the image file to exactly:
       photo2.jpg

STEP 4 — Copy both files into the assets folder:
   Paste both photo1.jpg and photo2.jpg into:
   C:\Users\PURNA - REVANTH\OneDrive\Documents\Kumar\assets\

STEP 5 — Open the website:
   Double-click on index.html (in the Kumar folder)
   Click the gift box → go to "Pics I Love" tab
   Your two photos will appear in glowing frames!

------------------------------------------------------------
NOTE: If your photos are .png instead of .jpg, that is fine.
Just make sure the name matches what you put in index.html:
   src="assets/photo1.jpg"  →  change jpg to png if needed.
   (Or just save your images as .jpg — easiest option!)
